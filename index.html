For

BEST helps technology students to develop themselves by learning new soft and hard skills, and to be more open-minded in order to understand different cultures. Through events made by students for students, BEST creates plenty of opportunities to meet and learn with each other and also provides the opportunity to improve the ability of team work.

For Companies

One of BEST Coimbras' biggest goals is to connect students and companies. To achieve this goal we’ve created several events throughout the years, the most notable ones are EBEC and BEW, to help companies reach the student community. In addition we also develop marketing partnerships that allow companies to share their work and career opportunities through our network.
